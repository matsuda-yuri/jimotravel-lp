What is Jimoto Travel?

Jimoto Travel is an app that lets you
quickly decide “Where to now?”
when you’re traveling or out and about.

Easy to use for sightseeing or local outings.

Make this city comfortable-
for everyone.

Jimoto Travel is an app built to ease overtourism.

Based on your interests it suggests city highlights and less-crowded times so foot traffic spreads naturally.

For visitors and locals alike
we aim for the city’s “just-right balance.”
